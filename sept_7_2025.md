# sept 7, 2025. in the morning. 

tethering is different from grounding.
i had several dreams about missing my mothers birthday. i did not get her a gift still. but we did talk on the phone. my sister too last month...
landmark days are piling up. the necrolog growing. the social security numbers keep coming. 
markers in time are grounding.
dates on bottles. food set to expire. what we did last year. forgetting that event i was just remembering forgetting. asked ian why we were at some guys house in bethel years ago tying knives to broomsticks.
i try to be untethered in some regards: flitting from social scenes, resisting becoming "a regular", traveling too fucking much. i try to ground myself in others: alone and alone but keep my tendrils up and out brainless and unblinking yearning for connection. 
i feel like i must be like a gulliver travel's giant monster. cold solid and sinking in some parts but otherwise full of hot air. pinned to the ground and bubbling up over the ropes grotesque flubber.
