## ingredients

fish (whiting)
nixtamalized corn - corn meal for tamales/tortillas
flour
egg
chili powder, ground cardomom, salt pepper
oil

mushrooms
heavy cream
a garlic clove

## method

mix the spices and the flour (1part) and the cornmeal (3part) together
pat dry the fish, egg the fish, flour the fish
fry the fish when the oil is at like 375.

in a separate pan cook down the mushrooms in a dry pan, you can remove them.
put some of the oil used for cooking the fish into that pan, it would be good to filter it so no burned parts get in. i just got the couple spots out with my spatula
slowly add the leftover spiced flour/corn to the oil making sure to stir it in
it should be like a saucey consistency. add more oil if needed. we are making a roux now. typically for gumbo you are supposed to drink like 5 beers here but we're just making a ... bechemel.
but do it until you're satisfied. add the msuhrooms back in and mix em up and around
add the heavy cream stirring as you pour it in. get it to a conssitency you like. you may have to addd water if it's too thick. the corn kind of took a second to soak up this stuff. spice to taste.
